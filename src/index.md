Hi, I'm a Computer Engineer from [Politecnico di Torino](https://www.polito.it),
specializing in software, and the former Head of
[Policumbent](https://www.policumbent.it)'s Systems Division, where we built the
[fastest](https://www.polito.it/ateneo/comunicazione-e-ufficio-stampa/poliflash/il-team-policumbent-vince-la-whpsc-2022-in-nevada)
human-powered
[handbike](https://www.lastampa.it/cuneo/2024/09/14/news/il_campione_paralimpico_diego_colombari_e_il_record_mondiale_di_velocita_in_nevada-14631143)
[in the world](https://www.polito.it/ateneo/comunicazione-e-ufficio-stampa/poliflash/il-team-policumbent-sul-tetto-del-mondo-il-racconto-di-un).

I completed my master's degree with a
[thesis](https://github.com/gabelluardo/tesi) on Rust, CUDA, and Vulkan for
[GPGPU](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/General-purpose_computing_on_graphics_processing_units)
programming. I'm also into web development, but my main passion is systems
programming with Rust and Go. I hope to publish my first indie game using
[Godot](https://godotengine.org).

I enjoy hacking and building smart 3D-printed ~~useless~~ gadgets with Raspberry
Pi Pico boards or ESP32s. I'm passionate about research and open source, and I
constantly explore new technologies and domains from a human-centric
perspective.

I also ended up building robots and rovers for the cleantech startup
[Reiwa Engine](https://www.reiwaengine.com/) in order to
_[make the world a better place](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=B8C5sjjhsso)_
from my Sicilian hometown.

Currently, I'm working at [Canonical](https://canonical.com/) as a Software
Engineer on [Ubuntu Desktop](https://ubuntu.com/desktop), helping to make the
year of the [✨Linux desktop✨](https://yearofthelinuxdesktop.net/) a reality.
